## Capture Feedback from Anywhere

Data & Enrichment integrations extend ProductBridge beyond your existing tools. Capture feedback directly from any webpage, enrich user profiles with external data, and bring in context that makes your feedback more actionable.

## Available Tools

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Chrome Extension" icon="chrome" href="/integrations/data-and-enrichment/chrome-extension">
    Capture feedback from any webpage with a browser extension. Highlight text, annotate screenshots, and submit feedback without leaving the page.
  </Card>
</Columns>

## How It Fits

Data & Enrichment tools complement your other integrations by adding context that communication channels and review platforms cannot capture on their own. Use the Chrome Extension to grab feedback from competitor reviews, social media posts, or internal tools that do not have a direct integration.

<Callout kind="info">
  All feedback captured through Data & Enrichment tools flows into [Feedback Intelligence](/core-concepts/feedback-intelligence) for automatic categorization and analysis, just like feedback from any other source.
</Callout>
