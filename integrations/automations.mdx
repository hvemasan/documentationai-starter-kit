## Automate Your Feedback Workflow

ProductBridge connects to the major no-code automation platforms so events in your workspace — new posts, comments, votes, status changes — can trigger actions in thousands of other apps without writing code.

Each platform page walks you through generating credentials from **Connect Sources** and building your first workflow.

## Custom Automations with Webhooks

Prefer to build your own? ProductBridge exposes generic **outbound webhooks** that deliver workspace events to any URL. Configure endpoints, choose which events to subscribe to, and verify deliveries with per-endpoint signing secrets at **Settings → Connect → API & Webhooks**.

Combine outbound webhooks with the [REST API](/api-reference/introduction) to build integrations with internal tools or platforms that aren't listed above.

<Callout kind="tip">
  Start with a single high-value workflow — like posting new feedback into a team channel — and expand once it proves useful. Over-automating too early creates noise.
</Callout>
