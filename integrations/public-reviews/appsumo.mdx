## Overview

The AppSumo integration imports reviews from your public AppSumo product listing into ProductBridge. Track what lifetime deal customers say about your product, monitor sentiment after launches, and analyze AppSumo feedback alongside your other channels.

Connecting requires **no credentials** — search for your product by name or paste your public listing URL, and ProductBridge handles the rest.

## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info" collapsed="false">
  - A public AppSumo product listing (e.g., `appsumo.com/products/your-product/`)

  - A ProductBridge organization with at least one feedback board configured
</Callout>

## Connect AppSumo

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Integrations catalog" icon="layout-dashboard" title-type="p">
    Sign in to your ProductBridge dashboard, click **Connect Sources** in the sidebar, and select **AppSumo**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find your product" icon="search" title-type="p">
    Search for your product by name and pick it from the results. Prefer pasting a link instead? Switch to URL mode and paste your public listing URL or its slug:

    - `https://appsumo.com/products/your-product/`
    - `your-product`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Optionally import historical data" icon="history" title-type="p">
    Toggle on **Import historical data** and pick a window between **1 and 30 days** to backfill existing reviews from that period. This is off by default.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Connect AppSumo" icon="check" title-type="p">
    The integration status updates to **Connected**. Reviews start syncing automatically — no further configuration needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How Reviews Sync

- **Automatic daily sync** — ProductBridge pulls new reviews once a day at **02:00 UTC**

- **Sync depth** — Each sync checks your most recent reviews (150 by default) and imports the ones it hasn't seen yet

- **Historical import** — If enabled at connect time, reviews from your chosen 1–30 day window are backfilled once in the background

Each imported review is tagged with its AppSumo source, sentiment is derived by AI from the review text, and the review flows into [Feedback Intelligence](/core-concepts/feedback-intelligence) for categorization and trend analysis.

<Callout kind="tip">
  AppSumo reviews often come in waves after deal launches. Since syncs run daily, feedback from a launch spike lands in ProductBridge within a day — enable historical import if you connect mid-launch.
</Callout>

## Disconnect

To stop syncing, open **Connect Sources > AppSumo** and click **Disconnect**. Reviews already imported stay in ProductBridge.

<Expandable title="Troubleshooting" default-open="false">
  - **Product not found in search** — Try the exact name from your AppSumo listing, or switch to URL mode and paste the listing URL directly

  - **No reviews importing** — Verify your listing URL or slug is correct and the product has published reviews

  - **Duplicate reviews** — ProductBridge deduplicates imported reviews automatically, so re-syncs never create duplicates
</Expandable>
