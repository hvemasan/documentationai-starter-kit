## Overview

The Capterra integration imports reviews from your Capterra product listing into ProductBridge. Star ratings are automatically converted to sentiment scores, and review text is categorized by topic — so you can track public perception alongside your direct feedback.

## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info" title="Before you start">
- A published Capterra product listing with at least one review
- Your Capterra listing URL
- A ProductBridge project to receive imported reviews
</Callout>

## Connect Capterra

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Integration Settings" icon="settings">
    Navigate to **Settings > Integrations > Capterra** in your ProductBridge dashboard and click **Connect**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Listing URL" icon="link">
    Provide your Capterra listing URL (e.g., `https://www.capterra.com/p/your-product`). ProductBridge validates the URL and displays your listing details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Import Settings" icon="sliders">
    Choose your sync frequency (daily or weekly) and whether to backfill existing reviews or start fresh.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activate" icon="check">
    Click **Enable** to start importing. ProductBridge begins pulling reviews immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Configure Settings

Customize how Capterra reviews are processed:

| Setting | Options | Description |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| Sync frequency | Daily, Weekly | How often new reviews are pulled |
| Backfill | On, Off | Import historical reviews on first sync |
| Sentiment mapping | Auto, Manual | How star ratings map to sentiment scores |
| Category filter | All, Specific | Import all reviews or only specific star ratings |

<Callout kind="tip">
  Enable backfill on your first sync to get a historical baseline, then switch to daily imports for ongoing monitoring.
</Callout>

## How It Works

Once connected:

1. Capterra reviews are imported on your configured schedule and tagged with "via Capterra"
2. Star ratings (1-5) are converted to sentiment scores: 4-5 = positive, 3 = neutral, 1-2 = negative
3. Review text is analyzed by AI to extract specific topics and feature mentions
4. All imported reviews flow into [Feedback Intelligence](/core-concepts/feedback-intelligence) for analysis
5. You can compare Capterra sentiment with other review platforms and direct feedback channels

<Expandable title="Troubleshooting" default-open="false">
  - **No reviews importing** — Verify your Capterra listing URL is correct and publicly accessible
  - **Duplicate reviews** — ProductBridge deduplicates by review ID; duplicates across sources are grouped automatically
  - **Incorrect sentiment** — Adjust sentiment mapping in settings if the default star-to-sentiment conversion does not match your expectations
</Expandable>
