## Overview

The Play Store integration imports Android app reviews from your public Google Play Store listing into ProductBridge. Monitor user sentiment, track rating changes after releases, and analyze feedback from mobile users alongside all your other feedback channels.

Connecting requires **no credentials** — you paste your public Play Store listing URL or your app's package name, and ProductBridge handles the rest.

## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info" collapsed="false">
  - An Android app published on the Google Play Store with a public listing

  - A ProductBridge organization with at least one feedback board configured
</Callout>

## Connect Play Store

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Integrations catalog" icon="layout-dashboard" title-type="p">
    Sign in to your ProductBridge dashboard, click **Connect Sources** in the sidebar, and select **Play Store**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paste your listing URL or package name" icon="link" title-type="p">
    Paste the public Play Store URL of your app, or just its package name:

    - `https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.example.app`
    - `com.example.app`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Optionally import historical data" icon="history" title-type="p">
    Toggle on **Import historical data** and pick a window between **1 and 30 days** to backfill existing reviews from that period. This is off by default.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Connect Play Store" icon="check" title-type="p">
    The integration status updates to **Connected**. Reviews start syncing automatically — no further configuration needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How Reviews Sync

- **Automatic daily sync** — ProductBridge pulls new reviews once a day at **02:00 UTC**

- **Sync depth** — Each sync checks your most recent reviews (150 by default) and imports the ones it hasn't seen yet

- **Historical import** — If enabled at connect time, reviews from your chosen 1–30 day window are backfilled once in the background

Each imported review is tagged with its Play Store source, sentiment is derived by AI from the review text, and the review flows into [Feedback Intelligence](/core-concepts/feedback-intelligence) for categorization and trend analysis.

## Disconnect

To stop syncing, open **Connect Sources > Play Store** and click **Disconnect**. Reviews already imported stay in ProductBridge.

<Expandable title="Troubleshooting" default-open="false">
  - **No reviews importing** — Verify the URL or package name you pasted matches your public listing and that your app has published reviews

  - **New reviews not appearing immediately** — Reviews sync once a day at 02:00 UTC; a review posted today typically appears within 24 hours

  - **Duplicate reviews** — ProductBridge deduplicates imported reviews automatically, so re-syncs never create duplicates
</Expandable>
