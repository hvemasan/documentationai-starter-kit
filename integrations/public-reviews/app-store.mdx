## Overview

The App Store integration imports iOS app reviews from your public Apple App Store listing into ProductBridge. Track what users say about your app, monitor rating trends, and analyze feedback alongside your other channels — all without manually checking the App Store.

Connecting requires **no credentials** — you paste your public App Store listing URL or your app's numeric ID, and ProductBridge handles the rest.

## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info" collapsed="false">
  - An iOS app published on the Apple App Store with a public listing

  - A ProductBridge organization with at least one feedback board configured
</Callout>

## Connect App Store

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Integrations catalog" icon="layout-dashboard" title-type="p">
    Sign in to your ProductBridge dashboard, click **Connect Sources** in the sidebar, and select **App Store**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paste your listing URL or app ID" icon="link" title-type="p">
    Paste the public App Store URL of your app, or just its numeric app ID:

    - `https://apps.apple.com/us/app/appname/id618783545`
    - `618783545`

    The app ID is the number after `id` in your listing URL.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Optionally import historical data" icon="history" title-type="p">
    Toggle on **Import historical data** and pick a window between **1 and 30 days** to backfill existing reviews from that period. This is off by default.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Connect App Store" icon="check" title-type="p">
    The integration status updates to **Connected**. Reviews start syncing automatically — no further configuration needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How Reviews Sync

- **Automatic daily sync** — ProductBridge pulls new reviews once a day at **02:00 UTC**

- **Sync depth** — Each sync checks your most recent reviews (150 by default) and imports the ones it hasn't seen yet

- **Historical import** — If enabled at connect time, reviews from your chosen 1–30 day window are backfilled once in the background

Each imported review is tagged with its App Store source, sentiment is derived by AI from the review text, and the review flows into [Feedback Intelligence](/core-concepts/feedback-intelligence) for categorization and trend detection — so you can track sentiment over time and correlate it with releases.

## Disconnect

To stop syncing, open **Connect Sources > App Store** and click **Disconnect**. Reviews already imported stay in ProductBridge.

<Expandable title="Troubleshooting" default-open="false">
  - **No reviews importing** — Verify the URL or app ID you pasted matches your public listing and that your app has published reviews

  - **New reviews not appearing immediately** — Reviews sync once a day at 02:00 UTC; a review posted today typically appears within 24 hours

  - **Duplicate reviews** — ProductBridge deduplicates imported reviews automatically, so re-syncs never create duplicates
</Expandable>
