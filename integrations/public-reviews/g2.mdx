## Overview

The G2 integration imports reviews from your G2 product listing into ProductBridge. Each review — including star ratings, pros, cons, and reviewer segments — is captured and analyzed alongside your other feedback, giving you a complete picture of public sentiment.

## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info" title="Before you start">
- A published G2 product profile with at least one review
- Your G2 product URL or G2 API credentials
- A ProductBridge project to receive imported reviews
</Callout>

## Connect G2

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Integration Settings" icon="settings">
    Navigate to **Settings > Integrations > G2** in your ProductBridge dashboard and click **Connect**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Product Details" icon="link">
    Provide your G2 product URL (e.g., `https://www.g2.com/products/your-product`) or enter your G2 API credentials for direct API access.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Sync Frequency" icon="calendar">
    Choose how often ProductBridge pulls new reviews:

    - **Daily** — New reviews are imported every 24 hours
    - **Weekly** — Reviews are batched and imported once per week

    Select whether to backfill historical reviews or start with new ones only.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activate" icon="check">
    Click **Enable** to start the integration. ProductBridge begins importing reviews immediately based on your settings.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Configure Settings

Customize how G2 reviews are mapped and processed:

| G2 Field | ProductBridge Field | Description |
|----------|-------------------|-------------|
| Star rating | Sentiment score | Converted to positive/neutral/negative |
| Review title | Feedback title | Used as the headline |
| Pros | Feedback content (positive) | Extracted as positive feedback |
| Cons | Feedback content (negative) | Extracted as improvement requests |
| Reviewer segment | User segment | Maps to industry, company size, etc. |

<Callout kind="tip">
  G2 reviews often contain both praise and criticism. ProductBridge splits "Pros" and "Cons" into separate feedback items so each insight is categorized and tracked independently.
</Callout>

## How It Works

Once connected:

1. G2 reviews are imported on your configured schedule and tagged with "via G2"
2. Star ratings are converted to sentiment scores and combined with AI-analyzed text sentiment
3. Reviewer attributes (industry, company size, role) are mapped to user segments
4. All imported reviews flow into [Feedback Intelligence](/core-concepts/feedback-intelligence) for categorization and trend analysis
5. You can compare G2 sentiment trends with feedback from other channels on the Intelligence Dashboard

<Expandable title="Troubleshooting" default-open="false">
  - **No reviews importing** — Verify your G2 product URL is correct and the profile is publicly accessible
  - **Missing reviewer data** — Some G2 reviewers post anonymously; these reviews are imported without segment data
  - **Stale data** — Check your sync frequency setting. Daily sync picks up new reviews within 24 hours
</Expandable>
