## Overview

The TrustPilot integration imports reviews from your public TrustPilot review page into ProductBridge. Monitor customer trust signals, track sentiment trends, and analyze TrustPilot feedback alongside all your other feedback channels in one place.

Connecting requires **no credentials** — you paste your public TrustPilot review URL or your business domain, and ProductBridge handles the rest.

## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info" collapsed="false">
  - A public TrustPilot review page for your business (e.g., `trustpilot.com/review/example.com`)

  - A ProductBridge organization with at least one feedback board configured
</Callout>

## Connect TrustPilot

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Integrations catalog" icon="layout-dashboard" title-type="p">
    Sign in to your ProductBridge dashboard, click **Connect Sources** in the sidebar, and select **TrustPilot**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paste your review URL or domain" icon="link" title-type="p">
    Paste the public TrustPilot review URL of your business, or just the domain:

    - `https://www.trustpilot.com/review/example.com`
    - `example.com`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Optionally import historical data" icon="history" title-type="p">
    Toggle on **Import historical data** and pick a window between **1 and 30 days** to backfill existing reviews from that period. This is off by default.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Connect TrustPilot" icon="check" title-type="p">
    The integration status updates to **Connected**. Reviews start syncing automatically — no further configuration needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How Reviews Sync

- **Automatic daily sync** — ProductBridge pulls new reviews once a day at **02:00 UTC**

- **Sync depth** — Each sync checks your most recent reviews (150 by default) and imports the ones it hasn't seen yet

- **Historical import** — If enabled at connect time, reviews from your chosen 1–30 day window are backfilled once in the background

Each imported review is tagged with its TrustPilot source, sentiment is derived by AI from the review text, and the review flows into [Feedback Intelligence](/core-concepts/feedback-intelligence) for categorization and trend analysis — just like feedback from any other channel.

<Callout kind="tip">
  TrustPilot reviews are highly visible to potential customers. Monitor negative reviews closely and use ProductBridge to track whether reported issues are addressed in your roadmap.
</Callout>

## Disconnect

To stop syncing, open **Connect Sources > TrustPilot** and click **Disconnect**. Reviews already imported stay in ProductBridge.

<Expandable title="Troubleshooting" default-open="false">
  - **No reviews importing** — Verify the URL or domain you pasted matches your public TrustPilot review page and that the page has published reviews

  - **New reviews not appearing immediately** — Reviews sync once a day at 02:00 UTC; a review posted today typically appears within 24 hours

  - **Duplicate reviews** — ProductBridge deduplicates imported reviews automatically, so re-syncs never create duplicates
</Expandable>
