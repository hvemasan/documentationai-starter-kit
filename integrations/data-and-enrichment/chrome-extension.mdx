## Overview

The ProductBridge Chrome Extension lets you create feedback posts from any webpage — competitor reviews, social media posts, support forums, or internal tools — without switching tabs. Feedback you capture lands on the board you choose in your ProductBridge organization.

Unlike other integrations, the extension runs entirely in your browser. There is nothing to connect on the server side: install it, sign in, and start capturing.

## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info" collapsed="false">
  - Google Chrome or a Chromium-based browser (Edge, Brave, Arc)

  - A ProductBridge account with access to at least one organization and feedback board
</Callout>

## Install the Extension

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add it from the Chrome Web Store" icon="download" title-type="p">
    Open the [ProductBridge Chrome Extension](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/productbridge/lpincafcclgbgncffebiemkmcaognpfo) page and click **Add to Chrome**. Confirm the permissions when prompted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in" icon="log-in" title-type="p">
    Click the ProductBridge icon in your browser toolbar and sign in with your ProductBridge account. Pick the organization and board you want feedback to go to.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify the connection" icon="check" title-type="p">
    Open **Connect Sources** in your ProductBridge dashboard — the Chrome Extension card shows as **Connected** automatically once the extension is detected in your browser. There is no manual connect step.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Capture Feedback

With the extension installed, click the ProductBridge icon on any webpage to create a feedback post. Fill in the feedback details and submit — the post is created in your selected organization and board immediately, and flows into [Feedback Intelligence](/core-concepts/feedback-intelligence) for analysis like feedback from any other source.

<Callout kind="info">
  The extension only sends data to ProductBridge when you explicitly submit feedback. Browsing activity is not tracked.
</Callout>

<Expandable title="Troubleshooting" default-open="false">
  - **Extension not appearing** — Check that the extension is enabled in `chrome://extensions`. Pin it to the toolbar for easy access.

  - **Card not showing Connected** — Detection runs in the browser where the extension is installed. Open your ProductBridge dashboard in the same browser profile that has the extension.

  - **Sign-in issues** — Ensure you are using the same email address as your ProductBridge account. Try signing out and back in.

  - **Submissions failing** — Check your internet connection and verify the extension is signed in.
</Expandable>
