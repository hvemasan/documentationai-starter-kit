## Bring Public Reviews into Your Feedback Loop

Public reviews on platforms like G2, Capterra, TrustPilot, and app stores contain valuable product insights — but they are often siloed from your internal feedback. ProductBridge integrates with major review platforms to import reviews automatically, so you can analyze them alongside all your other feedback in one place.

## Analyzing Public Reviews

Once imported, public reviews are processed by [Feedback Intelligence](/core-concepts/feedback-intelligence) just like any other feedback:

- **Sentiment analysis** — Star ratings are supplemented with AI-generated sentiment from the review text
- **Categorization** — Reviews are tagged with topics (Performance, UI/UX, Pricing, etc.)
- **Trend detection** — Spot changes in review sentiment or recurring themes over time
- **Cross-channel comparison** — Compare what users say publicly on G2 versus what they report through your portal or support channels

<Callout kind="tip">
  Use the Intelligence Dashboard to compare sentiment across public reviews and private feedback. Discrepancies often reveal issues users share publicly but not through support — or vice versa.
</Callout>
