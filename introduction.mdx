## Your Product Management Command Center

ProductBridge brings together everything you need to build products your users love. Collect feedback from every channel, let AI surface the insights that matter, plan your roadmap with confidence, and keep your users informed with a built-in changelog.

Whether you are a startup shipping fast or an enterprise managing thousands of feature requests, ProductBridge gives you a single source of truth for product decisions.

## What You Can Do with ProductBridge

<Steps>
  <Step title="Collect Feedback" icon="message-square">
    Gather user feedback from a public portal, in-app widgets, and automatic ingestion from tools like Intercom, Slack, and Jira. Every piece of feedback lands in one centralized inbox.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Analyze with AI" icon="brain">
    Use Feedback Intelligence to automatically categorize, analyze sentiment, and detect trends across all your feedback. Ask natural language questions and get instant answers from your data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Plan Your Roadmap" icon="map">
    Turn insights into action by linking feedback directly to roadmap items. Prioritize features based on real user demand and share your roadmap publicly or with your team.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish Your Changelog" icon="megaphone">
    Keep users in the loop with a built-in changelog. Announce new features, improvements, and fixes so your users always know what is new.
  </Step>
</Steps>
