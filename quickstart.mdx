## Prerequisites

Before starting, ensure you have the following:

<Callout kind="info" collapsed="false">
  - A modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari

  - A valid email address for account creation

  - About 5 minutes of time
</Callout>

No software installation required — ProductBridge runs entirely in your browser.

## Create Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Register for an Account" icon="user-plus" title-type="p">
    Visit `https://app.productbridge.com/signup` and enter your email, name, and a secure password.

    Click **Create Account**.

    <Callout kind="tip" collapsed="false">
      Use a strong password with at least 12 characters, including uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols.
    </Callout>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify Email and Log In" icon="mail" title-type="p">
    You land on the dashboard, where you can see your projects and recent activity.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Onboarding" icon="settings" title-type="p">
    Once you create an account, you need to complete the onboarding flow:

    - Enter your **Domain URL&#x20;**(Ignore if you don't have one)

    - Our AI automatically fetches the **brand details** based on the URL and auto-fill. Verify the details and proceed.

    - Select the list of **Feedback boards** you would like to start with.

    - Once done, we will scan the web to understand your brand and personalize the feedback items.

    Platform takes you to the feedback list view where you can manage all the feedback boards/items.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Set Up Your First Project

Once done with the onboarding, the next step is to configure things and add integration sources.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Configure Feedback Channels" icon="message-square" title-type="p">
    ProductBridge collects feedback from multiple sources. Enable the channels that work for you:

    - **Public Portal** — Share a link where users can submit feedback directly

    - **In-App Widget** — Embed a feedback widget inside your product

    - **Integrations** — Connect tools like Intercom, Slack, or Zendesk to auto-import feedback

    <Callout kind="tip" collapsed="false">
      Start with the Public Portal — it takes just one click to activate and gives you a shareable feedback page immediately.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Collect Your First Feedback

With your project created and a feedback channel enabled, you are ready to receive feedback.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Public Portal" icon="globe">
    Share your portal link (`https://yourdomain.productbridge.io/`) with users or teammates.

    <Callout kind="success" collapsed="false" color="#f59e0b">
      You can also configure your custom domain from Settings to access public portal on your subdomain (`https://feedback.yourdomain.com/`)&#x20;
    </Callout>

    They can submit feedback, vote on existing items, and add comments — no account required.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="In-App Widget" icon="code">
    Add the ProductBridge widget to your application with a single script tag:

    ```html
    <script>
      (function() {
        var s = document.createElement('script');
        s.src = 'https://app.productbridge.io/sdk/pb-widget.js';
        s.async = true;
        s.onload = function() {
          ProductBridge.init({
            organizationId: 'YOUR_ORGANIZATION_ID',
            position: 'bottom-right',
            defaultTab: 'feedback',
            buttonColor: '#3B82F6',
            buttonText: 'Feedback',
          });
        };
        document.head.appendChild(s);
      })();
    </script>
    ```

    The widget appears as a floating button in your app, allowing users to submit feedback without leaving the page.

    There are different options and settings available for Widgets at - [https://app.productbridge.io/dashboard/settings/widget-embeds](https://app.productbridge.io/dashboard/settings/widget-embeds)
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Integrations" icon="plug">
    Navigate to **Connected Sources** and connect your preferred tools. Feedback from connected platforms flows into ProductBridge automatically.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## View Your Dashboard

Once feedback starts flowing in, your project dashboard gives you an overview of everything happening:

- **Feedback Inbox** — All submitted feedback in one place, sorted by recency

- **Trends** — See which topics are getting the most attention

- **Sentiment** — Understand how users feel about your product at a glance

- **Roadmap** — Plan and prioritize features based on real user demand

<Callout kind="success" collapsed="false">
  Congratulations! You have set up ProductBridge and are ready to start making data-driven product decisions.
</Callout>
