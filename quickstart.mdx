## Prerequisites

Before starting, ensure you have the following:

<Callout kind="info" title="Requirements">
- A modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
- A valid email address for account verification
- About 5 minutes of time
</Callout>

No software installation required—ProductBridge runs entirely in your browser.

## Sign Up and Set Up Your Account

Follow these steps to create your account and access the dashboard.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Register for an Account" icon="user-plus">
    Visit `https://app.productbridge.com/signup` and enter your email, name, and a secure password.

    Click **Create Account**. You'll receive a verification email—check your inbox (and spam folder if needed).

    <Callout kind="tip">
      Use a strong password with at least 12 characters, including uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols.
    </Callout>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify Email and Log In" icon="mail">
    Open the verification link from your email.

    Return to `https://app.productbridge.com/login`, enter your credentials, and click **Sign In**.

    You'll land on the dashboard, where you can view your spaces and recent activity.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Initial Dashboard Setup" icon="settings">
    On first login, configure your preferences:

    - Set your timezone in **Account Settings > Profile**
    - Choose a theme (light/dark) in **Appearance**
    - Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) under **Security** for added protection

    Save changes to personalize your experience.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Create Your First Documentation Project

Now, build your first documentation space.

<Steps>
  <Step title="New Documentation Space" icon="plus">
    From the dashboard, click **New Space**.

    Enter a name like "My Project Docs", select **Documentation** as the type, and choose visibility (Public, Private, or Team).

    Click **Create Space** to generate it.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Your First Page" icon="file-text">
    Inside the space, click **New Page**.

    Title it "Introduction" and select **MDX** or **Markdown** format.

    ProductBridge auto-generates a starter page with frontmatter.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Basic Editing and Saving

Edit content using the built-in editor. ProductBridge supports live previews.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="MDX Editor" icon="code">
    Use MDX for rich components. Here's a simple example:

````mdx
---
title: Welcome
description: Your project docs
---

## Hello World

<Callout kind="success">Your first page is live!</Callout>
````
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Visual Editor" icon="edit-3">
    Drag-and-drop blocks for headings, images, and lists. No code needed.

    - Click **+** to add elements
    - Style with the toolbar
    - Preview changes in real-time
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Markdown Mode" icon="file">
````markdown
# Welcome (auto-converted to H1 in preview)

This is **bold** text with a [link](https://docs.productbridge.com).

- List item 1
- List item 2
````
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

Click **Save** or **Ctrl+S** to persist changes. Auto-save runs every 30 seconds.

<Callout kind="success">
  Congratulations! You've created and edited your first documentation space. Changes are live instantly.
</Callout>
