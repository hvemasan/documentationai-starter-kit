## Where to Find ProductBridge Updates

ProductBridge is a fully managed SaaS platform — there is nothing to install or upgrade. New features, improvements, and fixes roll out automatically to `https://app.productbridge.io`.

To see what has shipped recently:

- **In the dashboard** — Product announcements appear in-app as they are released.
- **On your portal** — Your own product's updates live on your portal's **Changelog** tab at `https://{your-subdomain}.productbridge.io/changelog`.

<Callout kind="info" collapsed="false">
  Looking to publish release notes for **your** product? See the [Changelog module](/core-concepts/changelog) to learn how to write, schedule, and publish updates for your users.
</Callout>
