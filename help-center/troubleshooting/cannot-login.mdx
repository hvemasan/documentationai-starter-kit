---
title: Cannot log in to dashboard
description: Resolve login issues on ProductBridge.
---

## Symptoms
- Login page reloads
- "Invalid email or password" error

## Quick Fix
<Steps>
  <Step title="Did you sign up with Google?">
    If you originally signed up with **Continue with Google**, your account has no password — entering one shows "Invalid email or password". Use **Continue with Google** on the login screen instead.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check credentials">
    Ensure your email and password are correct.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reset password">
    Click "Forgot your password?" on the login screen and follow the reset steps.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    Clear cookies/storage and retry logging in.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Dashboard vs. portal login

<Callout kind="info">
  The dashboard login (for your team) and your organization's public portal login (for your users) are separate. Portal visitors can sign in with email and password (minimum 8 characters), Google, or GitHub — or, if the organization has SSO enabled, they are redirected to its sign-in page.
</Callout>
