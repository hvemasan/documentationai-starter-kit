---
title: Cannot log in to dashboard
description: Resolve login issues on ProductBridge.
---

## Symptoms
- Login page reloads
- "Invalid credentials" error

## Quick Fix
<Steps>
  <Step title="Check credentials">
    Ensure your email and password are correct.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reset password">
    Click "Forgot Password" on the login screen and follow the reset steps.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    Clear cookies/storage and retry logging in.
  </Step>
</Steps>
