## Your Feedback at a Glance

The Intelligence Dashboard gives you a visual overview of everything happening across your feedback. Trends, sentiment, categories, and top requests are displayed in real-time charts and summaries — updated automatically as new feedback arrives.

You use the dashboard to spot patterns, monitor sentiment shifts, and identify what your users care about most without reading individual items.

## Dashboard Sections

### Feedback Volume

The volume chart shows how much feedback you receive over time, broken down by source (Public Portal, In-App Widget, Integrations). Use it to track engagement and identify spikes — a sudden increase often signals a release issue or a viral feature request.

<Callout kind="info">
  Toggle between daily, weekly, and monthly views using the time range selector in the top-right corner. You can also filter by feedback source or customer segment.
</Callout>

### Sentiment Analysis

The sentiment panel breaks down feedback into positive, neutral, and negative categories. Each item is scored automatically by AI, and you see the overall distribution plus how it changes over time.

| Sentiment | What It Indicates |
|-----------|------------------|
| Positive | Users expressing satisfaction, praise, or delight |
| Neutral | Feature requests, questions, or informational feedback |
| Negative | Complaints, frustrations, or reports of issues |

<Callout kind="tip">
  Watch for sentiment shifts after a release. A drop in positive sentiment or a spike in negative feedback can signal a regression before support tickets start piling up.
</Callout>

### Category Distribution

ProductBridge automatically categorizes every piece of feedback into topics like Performance, UI/UX, Pricing, Onboarding, and more. The category chart shows which topics dominate your feedback and how the distribution shifts over time.

You can customize categories in **Project Settings > Intelligence > Categories** to match your product's domain.

### Top Requests

The top requests panel ranks feature requests by demand — calculated from votes, mentions, and unique user count. Each entry links to the consolidated feedback view where you can see all related items.

<Steps>
  <Step title="View Top Requests" icon="list">
    Open the Intelligence Dashboard and scroll to the **Top Requests** section. Items are ranked by combined demand score.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Drill Into a Request" icon="search">
    Click any request to see all linked feedback items, voter details, and sentiment breakdown for that specific topic.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a Roadmap Item" icon="plus">
    Click **Add to Roadmap** to create a roadmap item directly from a top request. All supporting feedback is linked automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Filters and Segments

You can slice the dashboard by:

- **Time range** — Last 7 days, 30 days, 90 days, or custom
- **Source** — Public Portal, In-App Widget, Intercom, Slack, etc.
- **Segment** — Customer plan, company size, geography, or custom attributes
- **Category** — Focus on a specific topic like Pricing or Performance

<Callout kind="tip">
  Save frequently used filter combinations as **Saved Views** for quick access. Navigate to **Dashboard > Save View** to create one.
</Callout>
