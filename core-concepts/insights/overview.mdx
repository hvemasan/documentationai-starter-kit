## Your Feedback at a Glance

The **Overview** is the landing screen of Insights. It shows how much feedback you are receiving and how it is trending across every connected source — a quick pulse on engagement before you drill into themes, observations, and strengths.

## Filters

Every panel on the Overview respects the filters at the top of the page:

- **Time range** — the reporting window (defaults to **Last 90 days**)
- **Source** — limit to feedback from a specific source (Intercom, Slack, the public portal, and more)
- **Board** — limit to a specific feedback board

## Total Feedback

The **Total Feedback** panel charts your **daily ingest volume across all sources** over the selected window. It shows:

- the **total** number of feedback items in the window,
- the **window length** (for example, 90 days), and
- the **average per day**, drawn as a dashed line across the trend chart.

Use it to spot spikes — a sudden jump often signals a release issue or a feature request gaining traction — and to confirm feedback is flowing in from your connected sources.

<Callout kind="tip">
  A spike with no matching release is worth investigating early. Filter the chart by source to see which channel drove it, then jump to [Observations](/core-concepts/insights/observations) to read what people are saying.
</Callout>
