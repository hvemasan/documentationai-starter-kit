## Plan with Confidence

The Product Roadmap in ProductBridge is where insights become action. You create roadmap items, link them to real user feedback, and prioritize based on demand — not assumptions. Share your roadmap publicly to build transparency, or keep it internal for team alignment.

## Creating Roadmap Items

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add a Roadmap Item" icon="plus">
    Navigate to the **Roadmap** tab in your project and click **New Item**. Enter a title, description, and select a status.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Link Feedback" icon="link">
    Attach related feedback items to the roadmap entry. You can search by keyword, category, or feedback ID. ProductBridge also suggests relevant feedback based on the item's title and description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Priority" icon="arrow-up">
    Assign a priority level (Critical, High, Medium, Low) or use the **Impact Score** — an automatically calculated metric based on the volume and sentiment of linked feedback.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Roadmap Statuses

Organize your roadmap with clear status columns that communicate progress:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Under Review | Evaluating feasibility and demand |
| Planned | Approved and scheduled for development |
| In Progress | Actively being built |
| Completed | Shipped and available to users |

<Callout kind="tip">
  Customize status labels and add your own stages in **Project Settings > Roadmap > Statuses** to match your team's workflow.
</Callout>

## Linking Feedback to Roadmap Items

Every roadmap item can have feedback linked to it. This connection is bidirectional:

- **From the Roadmap** — Click **Link Feedback** on any item to search and attach feedback
- **From the Feedback Inbox** — Select feedback items and click **Add to Roadmap** to link them to an existing or new roadmap entry
- **From Intelligence** — Create roadmap items directly from the [Top Requests](/core-concepts/feedback-intelligence/dashboard) panel

When a roadmap item's status changes, users who submitted linked feedback can be notified automatically — closing the feedback loop.

## Prioritization

ProductBridge helps you prioritize with data, not gut feeling:

- **Impact Score** — Calculated from the number of unique users, feedback volume, and average sentiment of linked items
- **Effort Estimation** — Add T-shirt sizes (S, M, L, XL) or story points to each item
- **Impact vs. Effort Matrix** — Visualize your roadmap on a 2x2 grid to identify quick wins and strategic bets

<Callout kind="info">
  The Impact Score updates automatically as new feedback comes in. A feature request that gains traction will rise in priority without manual intervention.
</Callout>

## Public and Private Roadmaps

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Public Roadmap" icon="globe">
    Share a read-only roadmap page with your users at a URL like `https://roadmap.productbridge.io/your-project`. Users see planned features, current progress, and completed items — building trust and reducing "when will this ship?" questions.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Internal Roadmap" icon="lock">
    Keep your roadmap private for internal team use. Only workspace members with the appropriate role can view and edit roadmap items. Use this for early-stage planning before committing publicly.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
