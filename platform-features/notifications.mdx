## Stay on Top of Everything

ProductBridge provides a real-time notification inbox for both admin users and public (end) users. Notifications are generated automatically when actions occur across the platform — new posts, comments, assignment changes, roadmap status updates, and vote milestones — so you never miss what matters.

## Notification Inbox

The notification inbox is organized into three tabs, each showing a specific category of activity:

<Columns cols={3}>
  <Card title="Posts" icon="file-text" href="#posts-notifications">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Comments" icon="message-circle" href="#comments-notifications">
    Comments on your posts and replies to your comments.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Assignments" icon="user-check" href="#assignments-notifications">
    Posts assigned or unassigned to you (admin only).
  </Card>
</Columns>

Each tab displays an unread badge count so you can see at a glance which categories need your attention.

## Notification Categories

### Posts Notifications

Post notifications keep you informed about activity on feedback items across your project:

| Event | When It Fires |
|-------|--------------|
| New feedback post | A new feedback post is created in your project |
| Feedback linked to roadmap | A feedback post is linked to a roadmap item |
| Roadmap status changed | A roadmap item's status is updated (e.g., Planned → In Progress) |
| Vote milestone | A feedback post reaches 5, 10, 25, 50, 100, 250, 500, or 1,000 votes |

<Callout kind="tip">
  Vote milestone notifications help you spot trending feedback early. When a post hits a milestone, it is a signal that users care deeply about that request.
</Callout>

### Comments Notifications

Comment notifications fire when someone engages with your content:

| Event | When It Fires |
|-------|--------------|
| Comment on post | Someone comments on a post you created or are following |
| Comment reply | Someone replies to a comment you left |

### Assignments Notifications

Assignment notifications are available for admin users only:

| Event | When It Fires |
|-------|--------------|
| Post assigned | A feedback post is assigned to you by an admin |
| Post unassigned | You are unassigned from a feedback post |

<Callout kind="info">
  When a post is assigned to a team member, they also receive an **email notification** with the post details and a direct link — so they can take action even when they are not in the app.
</Callout>

## Email Notifications

In addition to in-app notifications, ProductBridge sends email alerts for critical events:

- **Assignment emails** — When an admin assigns a feedback post to a team member, the assignee receives an email with the post title, description, board, and a direct link to the post. This ensures team members are aware of new assignments immediately, even if they are not actively using ProductBridge.

<Callout kind="tip">
  Email notification preferences can be managed in **Account Settings > Notifications**. Team members can choose which events trigger emails and which are in-app only.
</Callout>

## Managing Notifications

### Mark as Read

You can manage your notification state in several ways:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Mark individual notifications" icon="check">
    Click on any notification to mark it as read. The unread badge count updates automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Mark all as read" icon="check-check">
    Click **Mark All as Read** at the top of any tab to clear all unread notifications in that category. You can also mark all notifications across all categories at once.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Filtering

Use the inbox tabs and filters to focus on what matters:

- **Tab filtering** — Switch between Posts, Comments, and Assignments tabs to see only that category
- **Read state** — Filter by unread only to see notifications that need your attention
- **All notifications** — View everything in a single chronological feed

### Notification Details

Each notification includes:

- **Title** — A human-readable summary of what happened (e.g., "John commented on your post")
- **Body** — A preview of the content (e.g., the first line of a comment)
- **Actor** — The user who triggered the action, with their name and avatar
- **Timestamp** — When the event occurred
- **Link** — Click through to the relevant feedback post, comment, or roadmap item

## Admin vs. Public User Notifications

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Admin Users" icon="shield">
    Admin users receive all notification types, including assignment notifications. Admins can also delete notifications from their inbox to keep it clean.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Public Users" icon="user">
    Public (end) users receive notifications for posts and comments relevant to their activity — such as comments on feedback they submitted or status updates on posts they voted for. Assignment notifications and delete functionality are not available to public users.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
