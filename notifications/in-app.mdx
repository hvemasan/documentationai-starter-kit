## Your Inbox at a Glance

Every team member has an in-app notification **Inbox** accessed from the bell icon in the admin dashboard. Notifications appear as things happen on the posts you follow across your workspace.

End users on the public portal also have a notification inbox, scoped to posts and comments they're involved in.

## Follower-Based Delivery

ProductBridge notifications are **follower-based**: you get notified about activity on posts you follow. You follow a post by clicking the bell icon on the post — and some actions follow it for you automatically. For example, **voting on a post auto-follows it**, which is how customers who voted for a feature hear about it when it ships.

## Four Tabs

Notifications are grouped into four tabs in the inbox: **All**, **Comments**, **Posts**, and **Assignments**. Each tab carries its own unread badge (capped at **99+**) so you can scan what needs attention.

## Posts

Notifications about activity on posts you follow.

| Event | Trigger | Who Gets Notified |
|-------|---------|-------------------|
| **Feedback Status Changed** | A feedback post moves to a new status | Followers of the post |
| **Roadmap Status Changed** | A roadmap item moves to a new status | Followers of the roadmap item (and authors of linked feedback posts by email) |
| **Feedback Linked to Roadmap** | A feedback post is linked to a roadmap item | Followers of the feedback post |

## Comments

| Event | Trigger | Who Gets Notified |
|-------|---------|-------------------|
| **Comment on Post** | Someone comments on a feedback or roadmap post | Followers of the post (excluding the commenter) |
| **Comment Reply** | Someone replies to an existing comment | The parent comment's author (excluding self-replies) |

Each notification includes a preview of the comment so you can decide whether to act on it without clicking through.

## Assignments

Assignment notifications are admin-only — end users don't see them.

| Event | Trigger | Who Gets Notified |
|-------|---------|-------------------|
| **Post Assigned** | An admin assigns a post to a team member | The assignee |
| **Post Unassigned** | An admin removes a team member from a post | The unassigned user |

<Callout kind="info">
  Assignment events can also reach you by **email** — control that per event in Channel preferences. See [Email Notifications](/notifications/email).
</Callout>

## Channel Preferences

Each team member controls their own delivery per event and channel:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Channel preferences" icon="settings">
    Click the bell icon to open the inbox, then click the gear icon in the header. The panel switches to **Channel preferences**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Toggle events per channel" icon="sliders">
    Under **Notify me about**, each event row has an **In-app** switch and an **Email** switch. Turn either off to stop that event on that channel. A dash means the channel isn't supported for that event.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="info">
  You'll only be notified about posts you follow — Channel preferences control *how* you're notified, and following controls *what* you're notified about.
</Callout>

## What Each Notification Looks Like

| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Title** | Human-readable summary — "John commented on your post" |
| **Body** | Preview of the relevant content |
| **Actor** | The user who triggered the event, with avatar |
| **Timestamp** | When it happened |
| **Link** | One click jumps you to the relevant post or comment |

## Read / Unread

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open a notification" icon="check">
    Clicking any notification marks it as read and opens the linked post or comment.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Mark all read" icon="check-check">
    Use **Mark all read** in the inbox header to clear all unread notifications.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Unread notifications are marked with an indicator dot; opening or marking all read removes it.

## Filtering

Switch between the **All / Comments / Posts / Assignments** tabs in the inbox header to focus on what matters. The list loads more entries automatically as you scroll to the bottom.

## Admin vs. End User Inboxes

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Admin Users" icon="shield">
    Admins receive every notification type, including assignments. Open a notification to jump straight to the relevant post or comment.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="End Users" icon="user">
    End users see notifications scoped to their own activity — comments and status changes on posts they follow (including posts they voted on), and replies to their comments. Assignment notifications don't apply.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## What's Not (Yet) Notified

ProductBridge intentionally keeps the in-app notification set focused. The following are **not** in-app notifications today:

- **New feedback posts** — new submissions don't notify the team; watch the inbox or moderation queue instead
- **@ mentions in comments** — no mention parsing or alerts
- **Moderation flags** — pending posts/comments don't trigger a notification (use the moderation queue instead)
- **Vote milestones** — vote-count thresholds don't trigger inbox notifications (a vote-milestone event exists only for the Discord integration)
- **Integration sync errors** — surfaced in the integration settings page, not the inbox

If any of these would meaningfully change your workflow, let us know.
