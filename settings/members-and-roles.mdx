## Workspace Membership

Every ProductBridge workspace has a set of **team members** — your internal users who manage feedback, integrations, and the public portal. Team members are distinct from [end users](/setup/end-users), who are the customers submitting feedback through your portal.

Each team member has one of four roles, granting different levels of access.

## Roles

| Role | What They Can Do |
|------|-------------------|
| **Owner** | Full access to everything. |
| **Admin** | Manage members and settings. Cannot manage billing. |
| **Editor** | Create and edit content. Cannot manage team or settings. |
| **Viewer** | Read-only access to all content. |

<Callout kind="info">
  Every workspace has an Owner — the user who created it. Owner is not an assignable role: you can't select it when adding a member or changing someone's role.
</Callout>

## Add a Team Member

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Team Members settings" icon="users">
    Go to **Settings → Organization → Team Members**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Add Member" icon="user-plus">
    In the **Add Team Member** modal, enter the person's email address and select their role (Admin, Editor, or Viewer).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add or invite" icon="mail">
    If the person already has a ProductBridge account, they're added to your workspace immediately. Otherwise, ProductBridge sends an email invitation linking to a sign-up page where they'll accept and create their account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track pending invites" icon="clock">
    Outstanding invitations appear in the **Pending Invites** section of the Team Members page. Each pending invite shows its **Expires** date so you can spot ones that are about to lapse. Use **Cancel invitation** to withdraw a pending invite.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="alert">
  Invitations expire after **7 days** and can't be resent. If a team member doesn't accept in time, cancel the old invite and add them again.
</Callout>

<Callout kind="info">
  Adding members is subject to your plan's seat limit. If you're at the limit, the **Add Member** button prompts you to upgrade.
</Callout>

## Invitation Lifecycle

Every invite moves through these states:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Pending** | Sent, not yet accepted |
| **Accepted** | The recipient signed up and joined the workspace |
| **Rejected** | The recipient declined the invite |
| **Canceled** | The inviter or another admin canceled before acceptance |

Once accepted, the user shows up in the members list with their assigned role.

## Change a Member's Role

Team members with member-management permission (Owners and Admins) can change another member's role.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the member" icon="user">
    Go to **Settings → Team Members** and find the team member in the list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update the role" icon="shield">
    Click the role dropdown next to their name and select the new role. Changes take effect immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

A few rules:

- Assignable roles are **Admin**, **Editor**, and **Viewer** — Owner can't be assigned from the dashboard
- Role changes require member-management permission, so Editors and Viewers can't change roles

## Deactivate or Remove a Member

Owners can temporarily suspend a member's access without removing them:

1. Go to **Settings → Team Members** and open the member's actions menu
2. Click **Deactivate Member** and confirm
3. To restore access later, use **Activate Member**

To permanently remove a team member, use the remove action in the same menu. Removed members lose all access immediately. Their historical actions (posts created, comments left) remain visible — attribution is preserved.

## Member Profiles

Every team member has a profile with:

- **Display name** — what other users see across the app
- **Email** — used for login and notifications
- **Avatar** — uploaded image or default initial

Members can update their own display name and avatar from their account dropdown.

## What's Not Currently Supported

A few capabilities you might expect that don't exist today:

- **Bulk invite** — members are added one at a time
- **Resending invites** — a pending invite can only be canceled; add the member again to send a fresh one
- **Per-board or per-project role scoping** — roles are workspace-wide; an Editor is an Editor everywhere in the workspace
- **Custom roles** — only Owner / Admin / Editor / Viewer; no custom permission sets

<Callout kind="info">
  Team members **can** control their own notification delivery. Open the bell inbox, click the gear icon, and use **Channel preferences** to switch each notification event on or off per channel (In-app / Email). See [In-App Notifications](/notifications/in-app).
</Callout>
