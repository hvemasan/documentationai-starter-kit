## Everything You Need to Close the Feedback Loop

ProductBridge covers the full lifecycle of customer feedback: collect it from every channel, make sense of it with AI, prioritize what to build, and tell users what shipped. This page maps out each capability and where to learn more.

## Collect Feedback

<Columns cols="2">
  <Card title="Feedback Boards & Moderation" href="/core-concepts/feedback-collection" icon="inbox" horizontal="false">
    Organize feedback into boards with statuses, tags, and categories. Review incoming posts in the Moderation queue before they go public.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Public Portal" href="/core-concepts/feedback-collection/public-portal" icon="globe" horizontal="false">
    A branded portal at `https://{your-subdomain}.productbridge.io` with Feedback, Roadmap, and Changelog tabs. Users sign in to post, vote, and comment. White-label it with a custom domain.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Widgets & Embeds" href="/core-concepts/feedback-collection/widgets" icon="code" horizontal="false">
    Embed feedback collection in your product: popup, sidebar, inline, custom trigger, iFrame, or a direct link. Identify logged-in users with a signed JWT.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Users & Segments" href="/setup/user-segments" icon="users" horizontal="false">
    See every end user who has submitted or voted on feedback, and group them into segments to analyze what specific audiences are asking for.
  </Card>
</Columns>

## Understand Feedback with AI

<Columns cols="3">
  <Card title="Analytics" href="/core-concepts/insights/overview" icon="bar-chart-3" horizontal="false">
    Feedback volume and trends across every connected source, filterable by time range, source, and board.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Insights" href="/core-concepts/insights" icon="lightbulb" horizontal="false">
    AI distills feedback into themes, observations, and strengths — deduplicated across customers and ranked by reach, with evidence one click away.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Ask AI" href="/core-concepts/feedback-intelligence/ask-ai" icon="sparkles" horizontal="false">
    A conversational assistant that answers questions about your feedback, roadmap, segments, and knowledge base in plain English.
  </Card>
</Columns>

<Card title="Knowledge Hub" href="/setup/knowledge-hub" icon="book-open" horizontal="false">
  Upload product docs, guides, and internal documents at **Settings → Organization → Knowledge Hub** to power AI answers with your own content.
</Card>

## Plan and Communicate

<Columns cols="2">
  <Card title="Product Roadmap" href="/core-concepts/product-roadmap" icon="map" horizontal="false">
    Turn feedback into roadmap items and prioritize them with built-in frameworks: **RICE**, **ICE**, **Impact/Effort**, and **MoSCoW**. Share the roadmap on your public portal or keep it internal.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Changelog" href="/core-concepts/changelog" icon="megaphone" horizontal="false">
    Publish release notes with labels and types, schedule posts, link them to roadmap items, and notify the users who asked for each feature.
  </Card>
</Columns>

## Connect Your Stack

<Columns cols="2">
  <Card title="Integrations" href="/integrations/communication-channels" icon="plug" horizontal="false">
    Ingest feedback from Intercom, Zendesk, Freshdesk, Slack, Discord, and Gong; import reviews from TrustPilot, the App Store, Play Store, and AppSumo; sync statuses two-way with Jira, Linear, ClickUp, and GitHub; automate with Zapier, Make, and Pabbly Connect.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Public API & Webhooks" href="/api-reference/introduction" icon="braces" horizontal="false">
    Manage posts, boards, comments, votes, and changelog entries programmatically. Subscribe to signed webhook events at **Settings → Connect → API & Webhooks**.
  </Card>

  <Card title="MCP Server" href="/integrations/ai-assistants/mcp-server" icon="bot" horizontal="false">
    Connect Claude and other MCP clients to ProductBridge. 22 tools cover feedback, roadmap, and changelog. Set it up at **Settings → Connect → MCP Server**.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Import Your Data" href="/help-center/guides/import-data" icon="upload" horizontal="false">
    Migrate from Canny via its API or bring data from any tool with the generic CSV importer at **Settings → Connect → Import Data**.
  </Card>
</Columns>

## Where Everything Lives

The dashboard at `https://app.productbridge.io` is organized into three sidebar sections:

| Section | Items |
|---|---|
| **Modules** | Feedback, Roadmap, Changelog, Moderation |
| **Intelligence** | Analytics, Insights, Ask AI |
| **Workspace** | Users, Settings, Connect Sources |
